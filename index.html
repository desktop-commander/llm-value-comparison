📐 How we calculate this

Not all tokens are equal — a token from a smarter model is worth more than one from a weaker model. We multiply raw token volume by model quality score (from independent benchmarks), then divide by total cost. The result: quality-adjusted tokens per dollar — one number that lets you compare a $3,500 GPU, a $20/month subscription, and a $0.07/M API directly.

🖥️ Local Hardware

tok/s × hours/day × 365 × years × quality %
hardware cost ($) = quality tokens / $

tok/s measured on your hardware. One-time cost amortized over years of use.

💳 Subscription

tokens/day × 365 × years × quality %
$/mo × 12 × years = quality tokens / $

⚠️ Token limits are estimated — providers don't publish exact numbers.

🔌 API (pay per token)

1,000,000
price/M (75% in + 25% out) × quality % = quality tokens / $

75/25 input/output ratio reflects typical real-world usage patterns.

Quality scores: AA Intelligence Index (0–100) from Artificial Analysis + Arena ELO normalized to 0–100 where 1000 ELO = 0%, 1500 ELO = 100%. Combined score averages all available benchmarks per model.
